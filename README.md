# Grytt – Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 1, 2026

Welcome to Grytt. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Your name, email address, and profile details when you sign up or log in using Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In.
- **Workout Data:** Workout logs, exercise history, saved workouts, and training preferences you enter into the app.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app performance and support.
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app, including features used and time spent, to help us improve the experience.

### Information Accessed with Your Permission
We only access the following device features when you explicitly grant permission:

- **Contacts:** We access your contacts list solely to help you find and connect with friends within the app. We do not upload or store your contacts on our servers.
- **Camera:** We allow you to use your device camera to capture workout-related photos or profile pictures within the app.
- **Microphone:** We use microphone access to support voice logging of your workouts, allowing you to log exercises hands-free.
- **Photo Library:** We allow you to select photos from your device to use as profile pictures or to save workout cards to your photo library.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- To create and manage your account.
- To track and display your workout progress.
- To help you find and connect with friends for social fitness features.
- To improve and personalize your experience in the app.
- To send you notifications related to your workouts and social activity (with your permission).
- To provide technical support and troubleshoot issues.

---

## How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited cases:

- **Service Providers:** We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics). These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety and security of our users.
- **With Your Consent:** We will not share your personal information for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

---

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption.
- We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.
- No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we will do our best to ensure the safety of your data.

---

## Your Rights

- **Access:** You can view your personal data within the app at any time.
- **Delete:** You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
- **Opt-Out:** You can revoke permissions (Contacts, Camera, Microphone, Photo Library) at any time through your device settings.

---

## Children's Privacy

Grytt is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
