Grytt – Delete Your Data

You can delete your Grytt account and all associated data directly from within the Grytt mobile app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Grytt app.
  2. Go to Profile / Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your user profile (name, email, etc.)
  • Workout logs
  • Activity feed interactions (nudges, cheers, comments)

What Data May Be Retained

We may keep anonymized or aggregated analytics data that cannot be linked to you.

If You Can't Access the App

Email gryttfitnes@gmail.com with the subject line: "Delete my Grytt account".
